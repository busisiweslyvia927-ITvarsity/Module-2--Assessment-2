The Origins of Ackermans Ackermans has been a favourite of South African shoppers since it opened its first store in 1916 after WWI by Gus Ackerman who was also the father of Raymond Ackerman. The first store was located in Wynberg South Africa, and in 1986, the Pepcore group acquired Ackermans. Today the company has more than 450 stores nationwide as well as across other African countries.
